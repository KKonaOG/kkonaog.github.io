Introduction

My name is Kyler Farrar, and I am majoring in Computer Engineering at Mississippi State University! As part of ECE 4723 (Embedded Systems), we are tasked to perform a dissection of an electronic product.

The product I chose to dissect is the Tiny Touch Tablet by VTech Industries. The reason I chose this product is primarily for three reasons: larger circuit board, multiple input technologies, and (due to its large size)

an easier reassembly process. The device itself features one trackball, nine touch buttons, a volume control switch, and auto-off functionality. All of these make a seemingly "simple" electronic toy interestingly complex!

The rest of this website is dedicated to documenting the results of the dissection. Primarily it will include:

  1. Product Information
  2. A Photo Journal
  3. Product Operation Details
  4. A Product Analysis
  5. Reassembly Procedures
  6. Lessons Learned
